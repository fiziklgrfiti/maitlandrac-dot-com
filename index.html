üéÅ About The Drive

What is a Reverse Advent Calendar?

Instead of receiving a gift each day, you give one! A Reverse Advent Calendar is a beautiful way to count down to Christmas by collecting essential items for families in need throughout our Maitland community.

How It Works

Each day in December, add one essential item to your collection box. Items can include non-perishable food, toiletries, children's items, or household essentials. At the end of the month, your box will be collected and distributed to local families who need support during the holiday season.

Making a Difference

Every box makes a real difference to families in our community. Together, we can ensure that everyone in Maitland has a brighter Christmas. Join hundreds of local families, businesses, and community groups in this heartwarming tradition.

What items should I include in my box? ‚ñº

Great question! We recommend non-perishable food items (canned goods, pasta, rice), toiletries (toothpaste, soap, shampoo), children's items (toys, books, clothes), and household essentials (cleaning supplies, toilet paper). Please ensure all items are new and within their use-by dates.

When do I need to register by? ‚ñº

Registration closes on [DATE TO BE CONFIRMED]. We have two collection rounds - Round 1 in mid-December and Round 2 just before Christmas. You can choose which round works best for you when registering.

How many boxes can I register for? ‚ñº

You can register for as many boxes as you'd like! Many families do one box together, while local businesses and community groups often register for multiple boxes. Just let us know the total number when you register.

Where will my boxes be collected from? ‚ñº

We have several convenient pickup points throughout Maitland. During registration, you'll be able to select the pickup location that's most convenient for you. We'll provide the exact address and collection times once you're registered.

Who will receive the boxes? ‚ñº

All boxes are distributed through established local charities and community organizations to families who have been identified as needing support during the Christmas period. Your generosity directly helps your Maitland neighbors.

Can I get involved in other ways? ‚ñº

Absolutely! We're always looking for volunteers to help with collection, sorting, and distribution. If you'd like to volunteer or if your business would like to become a collection point, please contact us after registering or reach out directly.
